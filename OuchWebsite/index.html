F.A.Q.

What is the process? +
Our process begins with a consultation where we discuss your vision, design preferences, and placement. We'll create a custom design, schedule your appointment, and guide you through aftercare to ensure your tattoo heals beautifully.
How you determine price? −
Size: Tattoo prices are often based on the size of the tattoo. Larger tattoos generally cost more than smaller ones. Some artists may charge per square inch or have set prices for different size categories.

Complexity: The complexity and intricacy of the design play a significant role in pricing. Highly detailed designs typically require more time and skill, leading to a higher cost.

Color: Tattoos with color typically cost more than black and grey tattoos. Vibrant colors often require more ink and skill, leading to a higher cost.
Can you draw any tattoos? +
Yes! Our talented artists can create custom designs based on your ideas, or you can choose from our extensive portfolio. We specialize in all styles from traditional to contemporary.
What is your location? +
We're located in the heart of Waltham Abbey, easily accessible by car and public transport. Visit us for a consultation or to view our portfolio in person.
